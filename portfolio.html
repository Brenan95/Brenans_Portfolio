The Inscrutable Box of Mysteries was a box label designed for a makeup company. It was used for boxes of makeup that had random products in it. The company was nerd theme'd so they wanted an 8 bit design.
This poster was designed to attract students to sign up for graphics classes. It was used as an example of the possibilities that come with using Adobe software.
Punny T-shirts with trippy backgrounds have been instyle for countless years. This design was for a T-shirt company that wanted a playful cartoony design with a funny play on words.
A beach resort needed a joke postcard that seemed just cheesey enough but still shared beautiful views of their resort. What better way to do that than giant squid tentacles coming out of the water?
Hotwheels come in many different shapes and sizes, and with the right photoshop skills its also possible to make your own. This is a picture of a real car thats turned into a hotwheel by shrinking it down and wrapping it in plastic.
This is another example of photoshop skill. These stom troopers were placed to make it look like they were duck hunting. The image was created for a T-shirt design.
Designing logos for certain companies can be difficult. Thankfully this logo was easy. This logo was for a fencing company based in Washington state. The client wanted to feature Mount Raineer in the design to incorporate a local theme as well as the colors similar to the Seattle Seahawks.
